Kid Nurty Music

Biography

Hailing from the rich cultural backdrop of Lagos, Nigeria, and now resonating through the energetic music halls of Denver, Colorado, Kid Nurty is a tour de force in the world of music.

About Me


A maestro with the pen and the mic, Kid Nurty skillfully bridges the gap between the storytelling traditions of his homeland and the eclectic sounds of his adopted city. With a repertoire that includes chart-topping numbers like "Alter Ego" performed by Yimeeka, and the groove-infused "Gbadun" by Jupiter Boy, [Artist Name] has established himself as a formidable musician and songwriter. His creations are a testament to his ability to transcend borders and genres, crafting tunes that are universal in language and sentiment. Kid Nurty has not only penned hits but also shared the stage with greats like Ghana's lyrical genius Sarkodie and Nigeria's soulful Adekunle Gold. Whether opening shows for these icons or headlining his own, his performances are electrifying experiences that showcase his dynamic artistry and deep connection with music. In the heart of Denver, Kid Nurty is a beacon of creativity, frequently spotted collaborating with local artists, lending his musical genius to uplift and enhance their work. These collaborations, supported by the visionary Cold Summer Record Label, have not only enriched the local music scene but also solidified Kid Nurty's role as a catalyst in the fusion of Afrobeats, international sounds. As kid Nurty continues his musical journey, every song he writes, and every performance he gives, is a chapter in a narrative that celebrates the unity and diversity of music.
