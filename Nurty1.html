About Me

My name is Harold Ayodeji, born and bred in the vibrant heart of Lagos, Nigeria, my musical journey is a tale as rich as the rhythms of Africa itself. I go by my stage name "ZaNurty", which I recently changed from Kid Nurty. Music flows through my veins; it's the legacy of my ancestors, echoing their dedication and the evolution they pioneered in African music. My passion has led me from the streets of Lagos to the scenic landscapes of Denver, Colorado, fusing the heartbeat of Nigeria with the diverse sounds of the Mile-High City. Over the years, I've had the honor of sharing stages with giants of the industry, opening for celebrated international artists like Ghana's iconic rapper, Sarkodie, and the mesmerizing Nigerian Afrobeat sensation, Adekunle Gold, among others. Every note I play, every song I sing, is a tribute to my roots, a nod to the past, and a bridge to global audiences.
